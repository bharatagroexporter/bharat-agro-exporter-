Steam

1401 Basmati — Variant 01

1401 Steam
Basmati Rice

1401 Steam Basmati Rice is one of the finest premium basmati rice varieties recognized for its extra-long grain structure, appealing aroma, and exceptional cooking quality. Carefully processed through modern steam technology, it preserves the rice’s natural properties, nutritional value, and grain strength while ensuring consistency in every batch. After cooking, the grains become long, fluffy, and separate with a soft texture and delightful taste. Due to its superior cooking performance and reliable quality, it is widely used by households, restaurants, caterers, hotels, and food industries, making it a preferred choice for domestic as well as international markets.

Grain Length 7.8–8.2 mm (average, cooked ≥ 18–20 mm)
Processing Steam Parboiled
Moisture Max 12.5%
Packing 1kg / 5kg / 10kg / 25kg / 50kg PP / Jute
Certifications GSTIN  |  FSSAI  |  MSME

1401 Basmati — Variant 03

1401 White Sella
Basmati Rice

1401 White Sella Basmati Rice is highly appreciated for its bright white appearance, extra-long grain structure, and superior cooking quality. Carefully processed using advanced white sella technology, it preserves grain strength, natural characteristics, and consistency in every batch. The parboiling process enhances nutrient retention while ensuring firm grains with excellent elongation after cooking. Once cooked, the grains become long, fluffy, and separate with a soft, non-sticky texture, making it an ideal choice for biryani, pulao, and large-scale catering applications. Widely used by households, restaurants, hotels, and food industries, it is a preferred option in both domestic and international markets.
